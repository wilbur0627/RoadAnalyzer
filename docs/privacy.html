Privacy Policy for RoadAnalyzer

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Introduction

RoadAnalyzer ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that analyzes baccarat road patterns for entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

We collect and store minimal information, all of which remains on your local device:

  • License Key: If you purchase a subscription (Ad-Free or Premium), your license key is stored locally in Chrome's storage (chrome.storage.local) for authentication purposes.
  • Game Data: Baccarat game results you manually input are stored locally in your browser for analysis and display purposes.
  • User Preferences: Your language preference and settings are stored locally.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal browsing history.
  • We do not transmit user data to any external server (except for license validation).
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We do not track your activity across websites.
  • We do not use cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our extension integrates with the following third-party services:

  • LemonSqueezy: We use LemonSqueezy as our payment processor for subscription management. When you purchase a subscription, your payment information is handled directly by LemonSqueezy. Please refer to LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.
  • Google AdSense: The free tier of our extension may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and tracking technologies as part of their ad serving. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

License Validation

When you enter a license key, the extension communicates with LemonSqueezy's API to validate the key. This is the only network request initiated by the extension (aside from ad serving in the free tier). The validation response is cached locally for 24 hours to minimize network requests.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. No data is stored on external servers. You can clear all extension data at any time by removing the extension or clearing its data through Chrome's extension settings.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
